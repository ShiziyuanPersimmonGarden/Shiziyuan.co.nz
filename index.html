Welcome to Shiziyuan Persimmon Garden

Persimmon picking starts April 25th, 2025 – bring your friends and family for a wholesome day on the orchard!

About Our Garden

Nestled in the countryside of Helensville, New Zealand, Shiziyuan offers a relaxing escape into nature. Our orchard is home to friendly animals, seasonal fruit, and peaceful scenery—perfect for all ages.

From curious ducks to calm cows and gentle doves, our farm family loves to meet visitors. Guided tours are available by request!

Our Services

  • Persimmon Picking: Fresh, sun-ripened persimmons straight from the tree.
  • Animal Encounters: Visit our ducks, cows, chickens, and doves.
  • Guided Tours: Learn more about the land with a guided walk (on request).
  • Picnic Areas: Bring lunch and relax among the trees.
