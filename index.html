Seasonal Persimmon Picking in Helensville

A peaceful, family-friendly orchard experience with fresh fruit, animals, and countryside views.

We're officially open for picking on the 18th of April 2026
$8 per kg — Pick your own fresh persimmons

About the Orchard

Shiziyuan Persimmon Garden is a privately owned orchard on Rimmer Road, Helensville. We open seasonally for visitors who want to pick fruit, enjoy nature, and experience a working organic orchard.

📍 Location
152 Rimmer Road, Helensville 0875
🌿 Season
April – June
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Friendly
All ages welcome

What You Can Do

🍊 Persimmon picking — $8 per kg

🍊 Pick fresh persimmons
🐓 Meet friendly farm animals
🚶 Guided orchard walks
🧺 Picnic and relax
