Welcome to Shiziyuan Persimmon Garden

We’re excited to welcome you to our official website! Persimmon picking begins on April 25th, 2025—come and enjoy a peaceful day out in nature with your family and friends.

About Our Garden

Nestled in the heart of Helensville, New Zealand, our Persimmon Garden on Rimmer Road offers a peaceful slice of countryside charm. Opening for the season on April 25th, the garden is a serene escape where nature and simplicity come together.

As you wander the property, you’ll find friendly ducks, cows, chickens, and even doves—creating a truly wholesome atmosphere. For those wanting a closer look, guided tours are available upon request.

Our Services

  • Persimmon Picking: Pick fresh persimmons straight from the tree and take home nature’s sweet bounty.
  • Animal Encounters: Get to know our farm animals including ducks, cows, chickens, and doves.
  • Guided Tours: Explore the orchard and learn about our land with a guided walk (on request).
  • Picnic Spaces: Enjoy a family picnic in a peaceful natural setting.
