Opening April 25th, 2025. Experience fruit picking, farm animals and nature at Shiziyuan Persimmon Garden, Helensville.

About Our Garden

Located on Rimmer Road, Helensville, Shiziyuan Persimmon Garden is a family-friendly orchard offering a calm countryside escape. Wander among fruit trees, meet friendly animals, and enjoy the beauty of nature.

What We Offer

Persimmon Picking

Pick ripe persimmons straight from the tree.

Animal Encounters

Ducks, cows, chickens and doves for all ages.

Guided Tours

Learn about the orchard and land (on request).

Picnic Areas

Relax with family in peaceful surroundings.
